Best Dramas According To Sireesha

My top three dramas of all-time

Strong Woman Do Bong Soon

It is a rom-com drama which fantansises a woman being strongest in the world and who decides to use her power to serve the people.

Twenty five Twenty One

It is a youth drama that talks about a group of yougsters who are going through a tough phase of their lives and still persists on chasing their dreams.

Vincenzo

It is a legal drama which starts with a man called Vincenzo Cassano, affliated to an italian mafia group goes back to south korea on a hunt of illegal money that was hidden by a passed away client of him. After a chain of events decides to fight against an organization's CEO who does vicious crimes in the name of development for the money. It is quite an interesting drama with a side of rom-com story in it.
